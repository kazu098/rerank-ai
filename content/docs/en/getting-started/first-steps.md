# First Steps

This guide explains the first steps to get started with ReRank AI.

## Get Started with ReRank AI in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Create Account (1 minute)

1. Visit the [ReRank AI homepage](https://rerank-ai.com)
2. Click the "Get Started" button
3. Register with your email address or Google account

### Step 2: GSC Integration (2 minutes)

1. Click "Add Site" on the dashboard
2. Select "Connect with Google Search Console"
3. Authenticate with your Google account
4. Select the site you want to monitor

### Step 3: Register Articles (1 minute)

1. Click "Add Article"
2. Enter the URL of the article you want to monitor
3. Enter the keywords you want to monitor
4. Click "Register"

### Step 4: Notification Settings (1 minute)

1. Open "Notification Settings" on the settings page
2. Enable email or Slack notifications
3. Configure notification conditions

## That's It!

You've completed the basic setup of ReRank AI. You'll automatically receive notifications when rankings drop, and improvement suggestions will be proposed.
