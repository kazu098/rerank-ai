# Auto Notification Settings

ReRank AI automatically sends notifications when rankings drop.

## Notification Settings

1. Open "Notification Settings" on the settings page
2. Select how you want to receive notifications:
   - **Email Notification**: Send notifications to your email address
   - **Slack Notification**: Send notifications to a Slack channel or DM

## Notification Condition Settings

- **Rank Drop Threshold**: How many positions must drop before notification (default: 2 positions)
- **Keyword Drop Threshold**: How many positions a keyword must drop before notification (default: 10 positions)
- **Comparison Period**: How many days ago to compare rankings with (default: 7 days ago)

## Notification Content

Notifications include the following information:

- URL of the article with dropped rankings
- Keywords with dropped rankings
- Summary of improvement suggestions
- Link to detailed analysis results
