# Manual Scan

Manual Scan is a feature that instantly displays current rankings and improvement suggestions by simply entering a URL and keywords, without requiring GSC integration.

## How to Use

1. Click "Manual Scan" on the dashboard
2. Enter the URL of the article you want to analyze
3. Enter the keywords you want to monitor (multiple keywords allowed)
4. Click "Start Analysis"

## Information Displayed

- **Current Ranking**: Search ranking for the specified keywords
- **Improvement Suggestions**: Bullet points of improvement items based on competitor analysis
- **Competitor Sites**: List of top-ranking competitor sites

## Important Notes

- Manual Scan retrieves real-time ranking data, but may be less accurate compared to GSC integration
- GSC integration is required to use automatic monitoring
