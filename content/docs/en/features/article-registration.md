# Article Registration

This guide explains how to register articles you want to monitor in ReRank AI.

## Article Registration Steps

1. Click "Add Article" on the dashboard
2. Enter the URL of the article you want to monitor
3. Enter the keywords you want to monitor (multiple keywords allowed)
4. Click "Register"

## How to Choose Keywords

- **Main Keywords**: Primary keywords for the article
- **Related Keywords**: Keywords related to the article
- **Long-tail Keywords**: More specific keywords

## Important Notes

- You can register up to 10 keywords per article
- The free plan allows registration of up to 3 articles
- Paid plans have different article limits depending on the plan
