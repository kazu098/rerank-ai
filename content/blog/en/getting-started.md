# ReRank AI Guide: Complete Beginner's Tutorial

The most time-consuming part of SEO is monitoring rankings and considering improvement suggestions. ReRank AI automates this process. This guide provides a detailed explanation of basic usage with actual screenshots for first-time users.

## What You'll Learn

- **Account Creation**: Register in 1 minute with email or Google account
- **GSC Integration**: Start automatic monitoring by integrating with Google Search Console
- **Article Registration**: Register articles and keywords you want to monitor
- **Notification Settings**: Automatically receive notifications when ranking drops are detected
- **Using Analysis Results**: Improve articles based on AI-proposed improvement suggestions

## 1. Account Creation (1 minute)

Creating a ReRank AI account is free and easy.

### Registration Methods

1. Visit the [ReRank AI homepage](https://rerank-ai.com)
2. Click the "Get Started" button
3. Register using one of the following methods:
   - **Email Address**: Enter your email address and password
   - **Google Account**: Sign in with your Google account (recommended, one-click registration)

### Why Google Account Registration is Recommended

Registering with a Google account eliminates additional authentication steps when integrating with Google Search Console later. It's also more secure as you can leverage Google's two-factor authentication.

### Post-Registration Verification

After creating your account, a confirmation email will be sent. Click the link in the email to verify your email address. Once verification is complete, you can access the dashboard.

## 2. Integration with Google Search Console (2 minutes)

The main feature of ReRank AI is automatically monitoring rankings by integrating with Google Search Console (GSC). This integration eliminates the need to manually check rankings.

### Benefits of Integration

- **Automatic Monitoring**: Automatically check rankings daily
- **Real-time Notifications**: Receive immediate notifications when rankings drop
- **Accurate Data**: Uses official GSC data for high accuracy

### Integration Steps

1. Click the "Add Site" button on the dashboard
2. Select "Connect with Google Search Console"
3. Authenticate with your Google account (OAuth 2.0)
4. Select the site you want to monitor

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if the site doesn't appear in GSC?**
A: Verify that site ownership verification is complete in Google Search Console. If ownership verification is not complete, the site will not appear.

**Q: Can I integrate multiple sites?**
A: Yes, you can integrate multiple sites. The number of sites you can monitor depends on your plan.

## 3. Article Registration (1 minute)

Register the articles you want to monitor. Registered articles will have their rankings checked automatically every day.

### Registration Steps

1. Click the "Add Article" button on the dashboard
2. Enter the article URL
3. Enter the keywords you want to monitor (multiple keywords allowed)

### How to Choose Keywords

- **Main Keywords**: Primary keywords for the article (1-2 keywords)
- **Related Keywords**: Keywords related to the article (3-5 keywords)
- **Long-tail Keywords**: More specific keywords (2-3 keywords)

**Example**: For an article about "SEO Strategies"
- Main keywords: "SEO Strategies", "SEO"
- Related keywords: "Content SEO", "Technical SEO", "Internal Linking"
- Long-tail keywords: "SEO Strategies Methods", "SEO Strategies for Beginners"

### Plan Limits

- **Free Plan**: Up to 3 articles, maximum 5 keywords per article
- **Standard Plan**: Up to 20 articles, maximum 10 keywords per article
- **Pro Plan**: Unlimited, maximum 20 keywords per article

## 4. Notification Settings (1 minute)

Configure settings to receive notifications when rankings drop. Receiving notifications allows you to respond quickly.

### Email Notification Settings

1. Open "Notification Settings" on the settings page
2. Enable "Email Notifications"
3. Verify the email address where you want to receive notifications
4. Configure notification conditions:
   - **Rank Drop Threshold**: How many positions must drop before notification (default: 2 positions)
   - **Keyword Drop Threshold**: How many positions a keyword must drop before notification (default: 10 positions)
   - **Comparison Period**: How many days ago to compare rankings with (default: 7 days ago)

### Slack Notification Settings (Optional)

You can also configure Slack notifications for team sharing.

1. Click "Slack Integration"
2. Install the Slack app
3. Select the channel or DM where you want to receive notifications

### Notification Content

Notifications include the following information:

- URL of the article with dropped rankings
- Keywords with dropped rankings
- Current ranking and previous ranking
- Summary of improvement suggestions
- Link to detailed analysis results

## 5. Reviewing and Using Analysis Results

When rankings drop, ReRank AI automatically performs analysis and proposes improvement suggestions.

### How to View Analysis Results

1. Check notifications on the dashboard
2. Click "View Improvement Suggestions"
3. Review the improvement items proposed by AI

Improvement suggestions include:

- **Competitor Comparison**: Elements that top-ranking competitor sites include
- **Missing Elements**: Elements missing from your article
- **Specific Improvement Suggestions**: Bullet points of items to add

### How to Use Improvement Suggestions

1. **Prioritize**: Address high-priority improvement suggestions first
2. **Improve Gradually**: Don't improve everything at once; improve gradually
3. **Measure Effectiveness**: Check ranking changes after improvements

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many articles can I monitor on the free plan?**
A: The free plan allows you to monitor up to 3 articles. You can register up to 5 keywords per article.

**Q: Is GSC integration required?**
A: It's required for automatic monitoring. It's not required for manual scanning. Manual scanning allows you to instantly display current rankings and improvement suggestions by simply entering a URL and keywords.

**Q: How often will I receive notifications?**
A: You'll receive notifications immediately when rankings drop below your set threshold. Rankings are typically checked once daily at 0:00 JST.

**Q: How accurate are the improvement suggestions?**
A: ReRank AI analyzes top-ranking competitor sites and identifies missing elements. Real user improvement examples show average ranking improvements of 5-10 positions.
