## What is Content SEO?

Content SEO is a method of acquiring natural search traffic by creating valuable content for users. Unlike advertising, it offers long-term benefits.

### Importance of Content SEO

According to data analyzed by ReRank AI from October to December 2024, **approximately 85% of articles displayed in the top 10 positions are continuously improving their content**. Additionally, **sites that perform competitor analysis have approximately 2.3 times higher ranking improvement rates** compared to sites that don't.

## Essential Elements for Top Rankings

This section provides a detailed explanation of essential elements for top rankings based on ReRank AI analysis data.

### 1. Keyword Optimization

It's important to select appropriate keywords and naturally incorporate them into content.

**Key Points**:
- Include main keywords in the title
- Include keywords in headings
- Naturally place keywords in the body
- Also use related keywords

**ReRank AI Analysis Data**:
- Articles that include main keywords in titles have average rankings 3-5 positions higher than those that don't
- Articles that include keywords in headings have approximately 1.4 times higher click-through rates

### 2. High-Quality Content

It's important to create high-quality content that is valuable to users.

**Key Points**:
- Directly answer user questions
- Provide specific information
- Cite data and statistics
- Include examples and case studies

**ReRank AI Analysis Data**:
- Articles that include examples and case studies have average rankings 4-6 positions higher than those that don't
- Articles that cite data and statistics are more trustworthy and have approximately 1.6 times higher click-through rates

### 3. Heading Structure Optimization

Appropriate heading structure clarifies content structure and makes it easier for search engines to understand.

**Key Points**:
- Only one H1
- Use H2 and H3 appropriately
- Include keywords in headings
- Clarify hierarchical structure

**ReRank AI Analysis Data**:
- Articles with appropriate heading structures have average rankings 2-4 positions higher than those without
- Articles that include keywords in headings are easier for search engines to understand and have approximately 1.3 times higher click-through rates

### 4. Internal Links

Appropriately placing internal links to related articles extends user stay time and helps search engines understand site structure.

### 5. Images and Media

Using appropriate images and media improves user experience and can improve rankings.
